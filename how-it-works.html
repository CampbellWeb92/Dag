How Dag Works

Browse, bid, contact and pay in a few simple steps.

1

Choose a breeder

Review each breeder’s profile, breeds, location, contact details and collection options.

2

Bid or buy

Submit a bid below the listed dog or choose the PayPal option when you are ready to pay.

3

Confirm collection

Contact the breeder to confirm health records, collection date, delivery terms and ownership transfer.

Important: Before publishing, connect bid forms to a secure database or email service and replace all demo PayPal links with verified breeder payment links. Always confirm breeder credentials and animal welfare documentation.
